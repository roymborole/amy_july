- How has the company's revenue changed compared to the previous quarter and the same quarter last year?
For Q2 2024, AES reported total revenue of $2.942 billion, a decrease of $85 million or 3% compared to Q2 2023 revenue of $3.027 billion. Compared to Q1 2024, revenue increased by $15 million.

The year-over-year decrease was primarily driven by a $185 million decline in the Energy Infrastructure SBU due to lower regulated contract sales and prices, lower CO2 purchases passed through, the impact of Argentine peso depreciation, and unrealized derivative losses. This was partially offset by a $55 million increase in the Renewables SBU from new projects in service and better hydrology.

- Is the growth in sales driven by volume or price increases? 
The decline in revenue appears to be primarily driven by lower prices at the Energy Infrastructure SBU, partially offset by higher volumes from new renewable projects placed in service.

The declining revenue at AES was primarily due to lower regulated prices and currency impacts rather than market saturation.
