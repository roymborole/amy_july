o How has the company's revenue changed compared to the previous quarter and the same quarter last year?
In Q2 2024, Abbott's total net sales were $10,377 million, an increase of 4.0% compared to Q2 2023 net sales of $9,978 million. Excluding the unfavorable impact of foreign exchange rates, total net sales increased 7.5% in Q2 2024.

For the first half of 2024, total net sales were $20,341 million, an increase of 3.1% compared to the first half of 2023 net sales of $19,725 million. Excluding foreign exchange, total net sales increased 6.3% in the first six months of 2024.

o Is the growth in sales driven by volume or price increases?
The sales growth in Q2 2024 and the first half of 2024 was driven by a combination of higher volume and pricing across several of Abbott's key business segments, particularly Medical Devices, Nutritional Products, and Established Pharmaceutical Products. However, sales were negatively impacted by lower demand for Abbott's rapid COVID-19 diagnostic tests compared to the prior year periods.
