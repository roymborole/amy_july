Revenue:
- Etsy's total revenue for Q2 2024 was $647.8 million, up 3.0% year-over-year.
- Marketplace revenue was $470.4 million, up 3.8% year-over-year, driven by payments expansion and GMS growth for the Depop marketplace, partially offset by a decline in Etsy marketplace GMS.
- Services revenue was $177.4 million, up 0.9% year-over-year, primarily driven by an increase in shipping label revenue, mainly from Depop.

o Is the growth in sales driven by volume or price increases? 
The growth in Marketplace revenue was primarily driven by volume from payments expansion and GMS growth at Depop, partially offset by a decline in Etsy marketplace GMS. Services revenue growth was driven more by price increases, particularly for shipping labels.

Etsy's modest 3.0% revenue growth suggests some market saturation and impact from the current macroeconomic environment weighing on consumer discretionary spending, particularly for the core Etsy marketplace. The slowing growth could negatively impact Etsy's stock price if investors were expecting stronger performance.
